Bao

* 300g (2 cups + 1 tablespoon) all-purpose flour, plus more for dusting
* 1 tablespoon cornstarch
(or a combination of both - what I usually do)
* 1/4 teaspoon salt
* 185g (3/4 cup + 1 tablespoon) whole milkn
* 15g (1 tablespoon) sugar
* 3g (1 teaspoon) dry yeast * 10g (1 tablespoon) safflower oil, plus more for brushing on buns

1. Cut the eggplants into three-centimetre dice.
2. Mix the sake, mirin, sugar and shoyu in a cup.
3. In a separate bowl, mix the red miso with four tablespoons of water to soften the paste.
4. Heat the oil in a large pan. When the oil is smoking, add the eggplant, fry for about eight to 10 minutes until tender.
5. Lower the heat and add the sake mix, stir for two to three minutes.
6. Add the miso and cook, stirring, for another couple of minutes.
7. Spoon on to a plate and sprinkle with coriander and sesame seed.
