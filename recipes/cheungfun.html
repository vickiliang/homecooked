Cheung Fun

* 2g Matcha (Powder)
(There are 3 main grades: culinary, universal and ceremonial.
Ceremonial being the highest quality!)
* 1/4 cup 80C hot water
(usually I just boil the water and wait for 5 minutes to use it.)
(feel free to add more water after you mix it)
* milk or any plant-based milk

Sift (if you can) Matcha into a bowl
Add ~60ml of 80C water
Briskly whist until frothy
(follow a pattern of "M" or "Z" to whisk )
Heat up milk/mylk (froth it too if you have a frother!)
Pour milk/mylk into the Matcha bowl.
Viola!
