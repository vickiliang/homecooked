Cheung Fun

* Rice flour 55g
* Wheat Starch 10 g
(If don’t have wheat starch, can use cornstarch to sub.)
* Corn Starch 5g
* Water 200g
* Oil 1 tsp
* Salt a pinch

Sauce (most important)!
* Peanut Sauce
Mix peanut butter with hot water until you get a good thickness of a sauce, definitely don’t want it to be too thick!
* Hoisin sauce
as sweet sauce: I usually mix a little hot water, so it is not as thick.
* Soy sauce
* Spicy sauce/oil
* Sesames (toasted preferably)
sprinkle on top!

1. Mix all the ingredients into one bowl
2. Boil water in a big pot
3. Find a plate that can fit into the big pot, lay parchment paper on the plate
4. Pour the mixture on the paper on the plate - just a thin layer
5. Steam for 2-3 mins
